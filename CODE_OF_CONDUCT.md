# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to foster a welcoming and respectful community.

## Our Standards
Be considerate, respectful, and collaborative.

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing the code of conduct.

## Scope
Applies to all project spaces and communications.

## Enforcement
Reports will be reviewed and acted upon by the maintainers.

## Enforcement Guidelines
Guidelines include warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal.

## Attribution
Based on the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1
